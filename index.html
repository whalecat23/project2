Arcane: A Deep Dive

A breakdown of what makes Arcane one of the most engaging and entertaining series ever created.

Art Music Story Voice Acting Real World Applications

Art

Arcane's art style was created by the same studio that does the cinematic visuals for the game, League of Legends, which the show is based on. Not only is the series extremely visually appealing, there are a ton of nods towards the game mixed in, along with an extremely engaging and thoughtful art style.

Music

The series features a central song for each episode, with contributions from major artists like Imagine Dragons, Twenty One Pilots, and Ashnikko.The music stands on its own, but is even better with context from the show. Each episode features one song, and the tones of each song match the emotion well. It is definitely a highlight of the show.

Story

Arcane’s story is an emotional rollercoaster, blending action, conflict, romance, and heartbreak into a compelling narrative. It is well written, giving characters depth and emotional intensity. It is one of those shows that you find yourself rooting for the "villains", and sometimes the heroes are complicated and multi-dimensional. The whole series does not contain any fluff, and it makes sense without any "plot armor" or non-sensical writing.

Voice Acting

The series boasts top-tier voice acting, featuring stars like Hailee Steinfeld and Ella Purnell, bringing characters to life with distinct and powerful performances.Each character seems to have th quality of being perfectly cast, with many of the characters boasting near perfect voices. The voice acting really hleps to carry the show.

Real World Applications

Arcane delves into real-world themes such as social class struggles, mental health, family, and political intrigue, making it deeply relevant and impactful.It is not preachy, but manages to be a relevant and valuable conversation all mixed into the world of Arcane's story.
